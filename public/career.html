About Career Adventures

Every business has an opportunity to impact the world in a positive way. Impacts are not only driven by data and financials, but with the courage to be creatively different. After graduating from Indiana University's Kelley School of Business, with majors in Finance, Entrepreneurship & Corporate Innovation, I decided to pursue a Master of Science in Information Systems. These experiences allow me to help empowering companies pursue out-of-the-box, yet financially realistic, technological solutions to improve their business processes. During my undergrad, I led as a Marketing Director for Kelley Student Government and as a Women In Business HR Director. These positions equipt me with skills to propose new initiatives, connect with people, and manage teams organizing multiple events. Providing platforms for my team associates to create and grow was the most gratifying element from my collegiate journey. As a take-charge personality who seeks change as opposed to waiting for it, I hope to apply the latter skills to my professional career. My career experiences as a Business Leadership Intern and as an Analyst Intern allowed me to solve real-world business problems. Looking into the future, I hope to continue my personal growth through job opportunities, especially those that join the analytical elements of finance, the technological piece of IT systems, and the innovations of entrepreneurship.
